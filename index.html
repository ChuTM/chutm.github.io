My Design Language.

My refined thoughts are truly astounding.

    While I'm not formally trained in art, my passion for UI design and illustration is undeniable. I've experimented with various tools and techniques, but Figma stands out as the best I've used so far.

    I'm particularly drawn to lines - their ability to blend, intersect, and thereby create a whole new dimension is fascinating. Coupled with my fondness for the blur effect, these linear backgrounds transform into dreamlike landscapes.

    The result is a design that's not only visually stunning but also neat and organized. It's a beautiful blend of aesthetics and functionality.
